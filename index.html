WR Research Group

WR Research Group, located in Taiwan, is committed to advancing research in water resources, dam engineering, and hydro-environmental systems. We collaborate across disciplines and regions to address modern water challenges and promote sustainable development. Our work integrates engineering, data science, and environmental analysis to support resilient infrastructure and global water security.
